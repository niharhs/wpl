How we’ll work together

Most projects follow a simple flow: discovery, concept, feedback, and final delivery. You’ll always see previews before anything is finalised.

1) Discovery – You share goals, references, and timelines. 2) Design – I explore layouts, colours, and typography. 3) Refine – We fine‑tune based on your feedback and prepare final files.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?
Most single posters or thumbnails take 2–4 days, while full sets or campaigns can take 1–2 weeks depending on complexity and feedback rounds.


What do you need from me to get started?
A short brief, any text you already have, your logo (if available), brand colours, and 2–3 visual references that match the mood you’re aiming for.


In which formats will I receive the final files?
You’ll receive exports in PNG/JPEG for sharing and PDF for print where needed. Editable source files can also be shared on request.


Can you help with ongoing content, not just one‑off designs?
Yes. If you need regular social media or thumbnail designs, we can set up a simple recurring arrangement to keep your visuals consistent every month.
