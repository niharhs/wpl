Selected Work

A snapshot of posters and visual pieces I’ve created for events, creators, and brands. Each project focuses on clear hierarchy, strong colour, and layouts that work across feeds and screens.

“We Are Hiring” Poster

A recruitment‑focused poster built with a bold central focal point and strong contrast, designed to stand out in crowded social feeds and notice boards. The layout keeps the main message loud while supporting details stay clean and readable.

Hiring Campaign • Poster Design

“Lahore” Concept Poster

A stylised poster exploring mood, texture, and typography around the theme of Lahore. The design plays with light, depth, and cinematic framing to create a poster that feels like a still from a story.

Concept Art • Poster Design

Spiderman Fan Poster

A fan‑art poster created to experiment with dynamic poses, angles, and comic‑style composition. The piece focuses on energy and movement, using diagonals and layered elements to keep the eye moving around the frame.

Fan Art • Character Poster

“Pragatha” / UI‑inspired Poster

A poster experiment combining storytelling and minimalist composition. The piece uses a limited colour palette, intentional negative space, and subtle texture to create a calm but memorable visual for a narrative‑driven project.

Narrative Poster • Minimal Design

“Hyderabad in Few Letters”

A typographic exploration where the skyline and landmarks of Hyderabad live inside the letterforms themselves. The design mixes photography and type to create a compact city story in one frame.

City Typography • Poster Design

“Kolkata – The City of Joy”

A poster celebrating Kolkata’s Victoria Memorial and iconic bridge, combining a warm sun‑washed colour palette with monochrome architecture. The contrast between the yellow disc and detailed illustration builds a strong, memorable focal point.
