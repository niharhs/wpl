About

A bit about who I am, how I design, and what keeps me inspired.

I’m Nihar Arya, a graphic designer who loves turning ideas into bold, clear visuals. I started designing by creating posters and social posts for college events, and quickly realised how much a single well‑designed visual can change the way people notice and remember a message.


Today I focus on posters, social media creatives, branding assets, and motion‑ready layouts for creators and small businesses. I enjoy balancing clean structure with expressive colour, so the design feels both organised and alive.


Design philosophy: every piece should communicate at a glance, feel aligned with the brand’s personality, and still hold up when someone looks closer. I like to keep my work minimal where it matters and playful where it adds energy.

What inspires me

A lot of my ideas come from film frames, music posters, and everyday scenes—street lights, reflections, colour combinations in random places. I’m inspired by designers who can keep things simple but unforgettable, and by brands that dare to use bold type and confident colour.
