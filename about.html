About

A bit about who I am and how I work.

I’m Nihar Arya, a graphic designer focused on bold posters and clean, modern visuals for brands that want to stand out. I enjoy turning ideas into designs that work across social media, print, and motion.


My toolkit includes Canva, Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, Premiere Pro, Blender, and After Effects. Whether it’s a campaign poster, a social media carousel, or a motion-ready layout, I care about clarity, hierarchy, and rhythm in every design.

  • Experience with posters, social media creatives, and branding visuals.
  • Comfortable designing for both digital and print formats.
  • Can work from rough ideas, references, or complete briefs.
  • Open to freelance collaborations and long-term brand partnerships.
